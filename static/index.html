Google+ Atom feeds

Want an Atom feed of your Google+ stream, ie posts from people in your circles, that you can subscribe to and read in your favorite RSS feed reader?

Follow these instructions to extract the three google.com cookies named SID, SSID, and HSID from your browser and paste them below.

Now add this link to your feed reader, and you're done!

>

Most things that aren't actual posts from your friends, e.g. X +1'd this post, are hidden. Let us know if you want to see them, and we'll consider it!

This app does not store or use your Google+ data, username, password, or cookie. It only reformats your news feed and passes it to your feed reader when requested. You can confirm by examining its source code.
